# Challenge

## Hackathon

- **Name:** AI Builders Challenge with IBM Bob
- **Organizer:** BeMyApp (organizer) · IBM SkillsBuild (sponsor)
- **Deadline / Demo time:** July challenge closes **July 31, 2026 at 11:59 PM ET**. Contest period July 1 – Aug 31, 2026. Winners showcased at a Virtual Conference on **Sept 16, 2026**.
- **Submission format:** Project page on the BeMyApp platform containing: team details, link to a **public GitHub repository** with a functioning prototype/PoC, and a **video (max 3 minutes)**. README must document: problem statement, solution description, AI approach, challenge theme, and how IBM Bob was used. **Each team member** must complete at least one IBM Bob learning activity on IBM SkillsBuild.
- **Event page:** https://aibuilderschallenge-bob.bemyapp.com/

## Problem Statement

We are entering the **July monthly challenge: "Reimagine Creative Industries with AI"** — build AI-powered solutions for content creation, storytelling, and creative tools.

(August theme is "Advance Space Exploration with AI"; a Wildcard track "Build Intelligent Systems for the Future of Work" also exists. We target July / Creative Industries.)

## Domain / Industry

Creative industries — design, content creation, marketing. Our angle: creative-asset version management.

## Judging Criteria

Four criteria, **equally weighted**, each scored 1–5 (max 20 points):

| Criterion | Weight | What judges look for |
|-----------|--------|----------------------|
| Technical Execution | 25% | "Effective use of IBM Bob and additional technologies, with a functional and well-structured solution" |
| Innovation | 25% | "Creativity, originality, and unique application of AI" |
| Challenge Fit | 25% | "Relevance to the challenge and ability to address real-world problems" |
| Implementation & Feasibility | 25% | "Practicality, scalability, and potential for real-world use" |

> Note: the hub page additionally lists **Real-World Impact** ("ability to create meaningful value and address real-world needs") as a fifth criterion. The Official Rules score four — treat impact as judged either way and quantify the pain in the video.

Prizes per monthly challenge: 1st $2,250 · Runner-up $1,250 · Most Innovative $750 · Best Use of Technology $750. Grand Prize $5,000 (total pool $15,000).
